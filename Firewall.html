What is a Firewall

Firewall works to protect users and their information from the bad side of the internet. Like a wall that burns the bad side away. It filters information thats coming through online and if it is flagged then it is not allowed through.

Firewalls use either one to three methods to determine if information coming through online will get flagged

  • Packet filtering - Packet or small chunks of data are tested through filters. If deemed worthy then it will be allowed through
  • Proxy service - Information online is retrieved by the firewall then sent back
  • Stateful Inspection - Compares specific key parts of the packet with a database of trusted information. If the data seems similar or reasonable enough then it is allowed through.
